Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic personal information such as your name, email address, and account details when you register on Contract Marketing Hub. This information is required to operate the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to manage your account, process contracts, handle withdrawals, and communicate important platform updates. We do not use your data for unrelated purposes.

3. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Data Sharing

Contract Marketing Hub does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may only be shared when required for platform operations or by law.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use basic cookies or local storage to maintain session state and improve user experience. These are not used for advertising or third-party tracking.

6. Email Communication

We may send important emails related to account activity, verification requests, or withdrawals. We do not send spam or unsolicited marketing emails.

7. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential. Contract Marketing Hub will not be responsible for losses resulting from unauthorized access due to user negligence.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform after changes are made indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the platform administrator through the official support channels.
